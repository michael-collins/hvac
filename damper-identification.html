What the panel terminals usually mean

Your reported wiring matches this: Zone 1 → M1 (white), M6 (red); Zone 2 → M1 (white), M4 (blue), M6 (red).

Safe 10‑Second Verification

  1. Turn system power OFF.
  2. Label current wires on Z1/Z2 M1/M4/M6.
  3. Power ON, then momentarily jump 24VAC from panel R to M6 with M1 as common: damper should OPEN.
  4. Momentarily jump 24VAC from panel R to M4 with M1 as common: damper should CLOSE.

Only do quick taps (1–2 seconds). Do not hold power on motors when end‑stops are reached.

Zone‑2‑Only Dehumid Application

During a dehumidify call, we want Z2 damper OPEN and Z1 damper CLOSED. The isolation relay will momentarily energize M6 (Z2) and M4 (Z1) to position the dampers.
