Wiring — BMPLUS‑3000 ↔ Ecobee ↔ GeneralAire (Zone 2 Only Dehumid)

Overview

Zone 2 (basement) Ecobee controls humidity. On a dehumidify call, the GeneralAire runs (D1↔D2 closed), the blower starts, Zone 2 damper opens, Zone 1 damper closes.

Assumes GeneralAire control terminals are D1 and D2 (dry contact), plus RF/GF/GT not used.
