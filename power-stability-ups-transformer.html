Power Stability — Small UPS + Dedicated 24VAC Transformer

Goal

Keep BMPLUS‑3000 logic and Ecobee thermostats powered through short brownouts so they do not reboot or misread inputs.

Recommended Topology

Only the low‑voltage control side is on UPS. Do not power the furnace or condenser from UPS.

Parts & Notes

  • UPS: 300–600 VA “desktop” type is plenty (router‑class).
  • 24VAC transformer: 40–60 VA dedicated to panel + stats + relay coils.
  • Tie all control commons together on the new C bus (panel, stats, dehumid relay).
  • Label the new R/C clearly to avoid mixing with equipment R/C.
